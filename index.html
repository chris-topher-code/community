About Cdiscovery

Connecting foreigners across China through authentic stories and shared experiences

Our Mission

Cdiscovery is a vibrant community platform designed to connect foreigners living, working, studying, and traveling across China. We believe in the power of authentic storytelling to bridge cultures, foster understanding, and create lasting connections.

Founded in 2024, we've grown into a thriving community spanning 156 cities with members from over 89 countries.

What We Offer

  • Share your China experiences and stories
  • Connect with foreigners in your city
  • Discover local tips and hidden gems
  • Get answers to your China questions
  • Join topic-based discussions
  • Access curated resources and guides

Community Guidelines

  • Be respectful and inclusive
  • Share authentic experiences
  • Protect privacy and personal info
  • No spam or self-promotion
  • Report inappropriate content
  • Help newcomers feel welcome

Get Involved

Want to contribute to Cdiscovery? We're always looking for community ambassadors, content creators, and volunteers to help grow our platform.
